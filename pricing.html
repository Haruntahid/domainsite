Pricing

Our pricing depends on the number of domains on your account plus domains waiting to be registered in your cart. The more domains you have, the less you pay. This is to cater for web designers/developers, hosts and ISPs who register domains for their clients through Name.co.zw. We also give you a 10% discount on the total amount of each domain registered for at least 2 years. To see a simplified price list specific to you, please log in.

Also note that for .co.zw domains pricing further depends on whether the owner of the domain name is a Zimbabwean resident or not. This is because ZISPA, the .co.zw registry, charges us additional fees per domain for domains registered by non-Zimbabwean residents.
